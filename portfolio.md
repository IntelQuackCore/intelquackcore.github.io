**Sam Gregg** is a Director of Photography and 1st AC local to Philadelphia. In addition to experience with modern cinema camera systems from RED, ARRI, Sony, Panasonic, and Canon, I also have experience 16mm and 8mm motion picture film cameras. I have also worked in live production environments with modern fiber broadcast systems from Sony and Panasonic. I have credits on narrative and unscripted sets, as well as over 250 bylines in the role of a video journalist. [List of modern and vintage gear I own is attached.](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1uJ9pmbqwla07XqOVKpPNCc3UcMvHipev543l-ObF4Yg/edit?usp=sharing) In addition to my time on set and in the field, I also have extensive experience in post-production workflows. I have worked on both scripted and unscripted projects in Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve, and Avid Media Composer. I have audio post production credits for work done in ProTools and Resolve Fairlight. If you have any questions about my work, equipment, or would like to work together, please reach out to me via email: **sam@quackcore.com**.
